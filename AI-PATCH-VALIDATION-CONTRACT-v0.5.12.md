# AI patch validation contract v0.5.12
Findings are `blocking`, `warning`, or `info`. Only selected `blocking` findings prevent commit. Update payloads require `id` and supplied mutable fields; they merge into the active-calendar authoritative record before complete-record validation. Unknown fields are never stored: harmless/export-only metadata is ignored with a warning; fields that imply unsupported mutation (including milestones) are blocking. The provisional transaction registry includes existing records, selected adds, and selected update targets, allowing same-patch references. The immutable plan is applied to a clone, revalidated, and persisted once; any failure commits nothing. No-op updates are informational and skipped.
